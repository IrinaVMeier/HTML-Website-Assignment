Teaching and Research Philosophy

My primary pedagogical goal is to teach students the strategies of engaging in the process of critical inquiry and to equip them with linguistic and cultural tools for the careers of their choice. Open dialogue, conscientious thinking, power of interconnectedness, and emphasis on the value of individual experience for the well-being of the local and global communities are the tenets of my teaching approach. My interdisciplinary research focuses on the development of Russian terrorism from the second half of the 19th through the 21st centuries and the birth of the modern terrorist in the Russian revolutionary period. I explore the philosophical contexts in which terrorism exists today, trace the development of terroristsâ€™ self-representation, and examine terrorism from the angle of cultural studies.
